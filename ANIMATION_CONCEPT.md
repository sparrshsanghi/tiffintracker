# Animation Concept

## Motion Philosophy

Motion should make Maa Sharda feel alive without making it feel busy.

The point of motion is not decoration. The point is reassurance, ritual, and clarity.

## Signature Motion: Tiffin Reveal

This is the product's signature act.

### Sequence

1. Closed tiffin appears.
2. A slight lift or latch release suggests readiness.
3. The top layer opens.
4. Meal components are revealed in sequence.
5. Steam rises softly.
6. The final open state settles.

### Emotional Meaning

- Closed means waiting.
- Opening means care arriving.
- Steam means warmth.
- Open means lunch is ready.

### Timing

- Total duration should feel around 1 to 1.5 seconds.
- The sequence should never feel slow.
- The reveal should feel special the first time and invisible thereafter.

### Rules

- Play on first open of the day.
- Play after meal change.
- Play after pull-to-refresh when the meal state changes.
- Do not replay on every visit.
- Respect reduced motion by jumping to the final open state.

## Steam

Steam should be treated as a warmth signal, not a special effect.

It should be:

- Soft
- Brief
- Gentle in opacity
- Never smoky or dramatic

Steam is the visual equivalent of saying the meal is fresh.

## Cards

Cards should move like composed paper or ceramic objects, not like floating widgets.

### Behavior

- Expand with calm vertical motion.
- Settle with minimal bounce.
- Use opacity and slight offset rather than flashy zoom.
- Avoid constant card churn.

## Timeline

The timeline should feel like memory forming, not a log updating.

### Behavior

- New events slide in quietly.
- Past events remain stable.
- Relationship memory should feel stored, not appended like a system log.

## Notifications

Notifications should arrive like gentle attention, not interruption.

### Behavior

- Use soft entrance motion.
- Stack cleanly.
- Avoid alarming bounce or shake behavior.
- Important changes should feel calm but unmistakable.

## Food Moments

Food should animate only when it strengthens the ritual.

### Allowed

- Lid lift
- Meal reveal
- Steam rise
- Soft highlight on fresh food

### Not Allowed

- Excessive wobble
- Cartoon sparkle bursts
- Repetitive looping motion
- Fancy transitions that distract from the meal

## AI Moments

AI motion should feel intelligent but grounded.

- Conversation chips can glide in with a mild ease-out.
- Suggestions should appear like helpful notes, not popups.
- Confirmation states should feel like a confident handoff.

## Micro Motion Language

- Small actions should feel immediate.
- State changes should be legible.
- Completion should feel soft, not celebratory in a loud way.
- The product should breathe rather than bounce.

## Reduced Motion

When reduced motion is on:

- Use direct state changes.
- Keep the tiffin open when possible.
- Preserve meaning through layout and copy.
- Never remove the signifier of freshness or completion.

## Motion Summary

The ideal motion language is calm, food-led, and ritualistic.

It should make the product feel cared for, like a routine that has been repeated enough times to become trustworthy.
