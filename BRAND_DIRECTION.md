# Brand Direction

## Brand Statement

Maa Sharda is the AI-first operating system for a home-style tiffin service.

The brand promise is simple:

Your lunch is taken care of.

## Brand Personality

Maa Sharda should feel:

- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Premium
- Homemade
- Human
- Helpful

It should never feel corporate, fintech, or generic SaaS.

## Brand Voice

### Customer Voice

Speak like someone who already knows the household routine.

- Short
- Reassuring
- Human
- Clear
- Never technical

The customer should feel cared for, not processed.

### Manager Voice

Speak like an organized operator.

- Direct
- Status-led
- Decisive
- Concise
- Action-oriented

The manager experience should sound like work, but not like a cold enterprise system.

## Brand Metaphor

The tiffin is not an icon.

It is the mascot.

It should carry emotional weight across the product, especially in Today and in motion moments.

## Trust Signals

The brand should repeatedly reinforce trust through:

- Daily ritual consistency
- Clear delivery status
- Helpful AI with memory
- Simple approvals
- Calm payment communication
- Relationship history framed as memory

## Brand Boundaries

The brand should avoid:

- Loud gradients as decoration
- Financial language
- Overly playful mascot behavior
- Generic chatbot language
- Chat bubble chaos
- Dense admin visual patterns in customer surfaces

## Customer Promise

The customer should feel that Maa Sharda remembers them, respects their routine, and takes care of the small daily decisions that make lunch feel dependable.

## Recommended Direction

The primary brand expression should be Modern Indian with strong restraint.

The visuals should carry enough warmth to feel homemade, while the structure carries enough clarity to feel premium and trusted.

If the brand becomes too plain, it loses soul.

If it becomes too decorative, it loses confidence.

The target is a calm middle ground with a distinctly Indian heart.
