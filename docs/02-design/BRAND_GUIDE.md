# Brand Guide

## Purpose

Maa Sharda should be recognizable from one screenshot: warm tiffin service, personal trust, and premium daily care.

## Brand Feeling

Customer experience:

- Warm
- Reassuring
- Personal
- Food-first
- Low effort

Owner experience:

- Fast
- Dense
- Decisive
- Operational
- Clear about what needs action

## Signature Signals

Maa Sharda should be visually remembered through:

- Warm tamarind and saffron brand color.
- Green trust and delivery completion accents.
- The daily tiffin reveal.
- Relationship timeline language.
- WhatsApp-like AI conversation.
- Bottom navigation with no Home tab.

## Customer Screens

The customer app opens directly to Today.

Default first screen:

- `Good Evening 👋`
- Today's Meal
- Animated tiffin
- Meal items
- ETA
- Delivery Partner
- Pause Meal

No customer screen should feel like a table, ledger, or admin panel.

## Owner Screens

The owner app is a work console.

Default first screen:

- Dashboard KPIs
- Revenue
- Deliveries
- Collections
- Pending approvals
- Quick actions

Owner screens optimize speed over comfort. Dense information is allowed when it helps decision-making.

## Copy Voice

Customer copy:

- Short
- Human
- Reassuring
- Never technical

Owner copy:

- Direct
- Status-led
- Action-oriented
- Clear about consequences

## Illustration Style

Use product-tied illustrations only.

Allowed:

- Tiffin stack
- Steam
- Route/rider
- Payment tiffin click
- Approval stamp

Avoid:

- Generic food clip art
- Decorative blobs
- Stock-like happy people
- Abstract gradients as the main identity

## Empty States

Empty states should be quiet and useful.

Customer examples:

- History: relationship events will appear after deliveries, payments, and requests.
- Notifications: no new updates.
- AI: show natural language suggestions.

Owner examples:

- AI Inbox: no pending approvals.
- Operations: no active exceptions.
- Payments: no payments due in this filter.

## Screenshots

The strongest demo screenshots should show:

1. Customer Today with open tiffin.
2. Customer AI conversation with suggestions.
3. Customer History as relationship memory.
4. Owner Dashboard with KPIs and pending approvals.
5. Owner AI approval card.
